Mango Salsa

Description

Mango salsa is disgusting but we have provided this recipe for any absolutely sickos out there.

Ingredients

  • One mango - peeled, seeded, and chopped
  • 1/4 cup finely chopped red bell pepper
  • One scallion, chopped
  • 1 fresh jalepeno, finely chopped
  • 2 tbsp chopped cilantro
  • 2 tbsp lime juice
  • 1 tbsp lemon juice

Steps

  1. Gather ingredients (obviously).
  2. Place mango, red bell pepper, green onion, jalapeno, cilantro, lime juice, and lemon juice in a medium bowl.
  3. Mix ingredients well to combine. Cover and let sit at least 30 minutes before serving.
  4. Enjoy! (optional)
